Pilates

Pilates is a physical fitness system developed in the early 20th century by Joseph Pilates.It is a form of low-impact exercise that aims to strengthen muscles while improving postural alignment and flexibility. Pilates moves tend to target the core, although the exercises work other areas of your body as well. You can do Pilates with or without equipment.




Yoga

Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India. The purpose of yoga is to build strength, awareness and harmony in both the mind and body. It's a total mind-body workout that combines strengthening and stretching poses with deep breathing and meditation or relaxation. There are more than 100 different forms of yoga.




Barre

Barre is a form of physical exercise, usually conducted in group classes in gyms or specialty studios.It is a hybrid workout class - combining ballet-inspired moves with elements of Pilates, dance, yoga and strength training. Most classes incorporate a ballet barre and use classic dance moves such as plies, alongside static stretches. Barre also focuses on high reps of small range movements.




Strength Training

Strength training, also called weight training or resistance training, is an important part of any fitness routine. It helps make you stronger and also builds muscle endurance. With strength training, you move your body against some type of resistance, such as: your body weight. free weights, like dumbbells or barbells. Some forms of exercises are : Squats, Deadlift, Glute Bridge, Push-Up, Bent-Over Row, Hollow-Body Hold, Single-Leg Moves and many more.
